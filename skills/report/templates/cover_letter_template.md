# Cover Letter 模板 (投稿信模板)

> 适用于 NEJM / Lancet / JAMA / BMJ 等高影响力期刊投稿
> 版本: v1.0.0 | 更新: 2026-06-26

---

## 标准 Cover Letter 模板

```
[日期: YYYY-MM-DD]

[Editor-in-Chief Name, 学位]
Editor-in-Chief
[Journal Name]

Dear Dr./Prof. [Last Name],

# 投稿声明

We would like to submit our manuscript entitled "[Full Title]" for consideration as a [Article Type, e.g., Original Research / Review / Brief Communication] in [Journal Name].

# 研究概述（2-3句话）

[用2-3句话概括研究背景、方法、主要发现。例如:]
[研究背景一句话] + [方法一句话] + [关键结果一句话，包含主要效应量和P值]

# 研究新颖性（1-2句话）

This study is novel because [说明1-2个核心创新点，如首次报告、最大样本量、新方法等]。

# 临床/科学意义（1-2句话）

These findings have important implications for [临床实践/政策制定/未来研究方向]。

# 期刊适配性（1句话）

We believe this manuscript is well-suited for [Journal Name] because [说明为何适合该期刊的读者群和范围]。

# 伦理与合规声明

- This study was approved by [伦理委员会名称] (Approval No. [编号]).
- All participants provided written informed consent.
- This study was conducted in accordance with the Declaration of Helsinki.
- [如适用] The study was registered at [注册平台] (Registration No. [编号])。

# 利益冲突与披露

- All authors declare no conflicts of interest.
- This study was funded by [资助机构] (Grant No. [编号]). The funder had no role in study design, data collection, analysis, interpretation, or manuscript preparation.
- [如有] All authors have completed the ICMJE uniform disclosure form.

# 作者确认

All authors have read and approved the final manuscript. All authors meet the ICMJE criteria for authorship.

# 数据可用性

[数据可用性声明，如:]
- The data that support the findings of this study are available from [数据源] upon reasonable request.
- [或] The datasets generated and/or analyzed during the current study are available in the [Repository Name] repository, [URL].

# 审稿人建议（如期刊要求）

We suggest the following potential reviewers:
1. [Name], [Institution], [Email] — expertise in [领域]
2. [Name], [Institution], [Email] — expertise in [领域]
3. [Name], [Institution], [Email] — expertise in [领域]

[如有] We request that the following individuals not be considered as reviewers due to potential conflicts of interest: [Name(s) and reason(s)].

# 补充信息

- Word count: [正文词数] / [期刊限制]
- Number of tables: [N]
- Number of figures: [N]
- Number of supplementary files: [N]

Thank you for considering our manuscript. We look forward to your response.

Sincerely,

[通讯作者姓名], [学位]
[职位]
[单位]
[邮箱]
[电话]
```

## 各期刊 Cover Letter 特殊要求

| 期刊 | 特殊要求 | 注意事项 |
|------|---------|---------|
| NEJM | 需说明"新颖性对临床实践的影响" | 强调临床实践改变潜力 |
| Lancet | 需说明"全球健康相关性" | 强调对中低收入国家的意义 |
| JAMA | 需声明"所有作者已阅读ICMJE标准" | 严格遵循ICMJE要求 |
| BMJ | 可在线提交简短cover letter | 不超过300词 |
| Annals | 需说明"对内科医生的价值" | 强调临床决策影响 |
| PLOS Medicine | 需包含"作者摘要" | 用通俗语言概括 |

## Cover Letter 写作常见错误

| 错误 | 正确做法 |
|------|---------|
| 复制粘贴摘要 | 用2-3句话精炼概括核心发现 |
| 过度夸大（"突破性发现"） | 客观陈述新颖性和意义 |
| 遗漏伦理声明 | 每篇必须包含伦理批准和知情同意 |
| 遗漏利益冲突 | 即使无冲突也需明确声明 |
| 忘记签名 | 通讯作者手签或电子签名 |
| 未提及文章类型 | 明确说明投稿类型（Original Research/Review等） |
